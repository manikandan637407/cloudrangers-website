Project Overview

The Smart Ration Distribution System is designed to enhance efficiency, transparency, and security in distributing essential commodities such as rice, wheat, sugar, and oil.

It employs IoT technology to enable real-time monitoring and control of stock levels, ensuring accurate and fair distribution. The system features a large cylindrical drum for storing commodities, and customers can purchase items using their ration cards.

Automated weighing, tamper detection, and alert mechanisms ensure a secure and reliable process.

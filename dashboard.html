Tamil Nadu Ration System

The Smart Ration Distribution System is designed to enhance efficiency, transparency, and security in the distribution of essential commodities such as rice, wheat, sugar, and oil. It utilizes IoT technology for real-time stock monitoring, automated weighing with load cells, and security features like tamper detection. Alerts are triggered for unauthorized access, and notifications are sent to stakeholders via calls and SMS. This automation reduces manual supervision, improves response time, and ensures fair distribution across ration shops.
